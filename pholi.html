A Mission for Nature and Wellness.

Prithvi Resorts And Holiday Pvt Ltd is a leading provider of exceptional holiday experiences in India. Our mission is to offer bespoke holiday packages that bring people closer to nature, culture, and wellness.

As a premier hospitality group, we serve as the parent company to a curated portfolio of resorts. We focus on delivering personalized experiences that cater to all kinds of travelers, from families to corporate groups, working tirelessly to create unforgettable memories in some of the country’s most beautiful and serene locations.
